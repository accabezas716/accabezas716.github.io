Projects

XPHERE

Xphere is a type of action / platform game, within the category of "endless runner". This is really fun because challenge your ability to overcome the different obstacles, which will take you on a journey through the immensity of the universe. Thus, you will know universal phenomena very close to you, like; The existence of colossal celestial bodies, the great red spot of Jupiter, the "worm holes", large nebulae, the asteroid belt of the solar system, among others. On the other hand, there will be tools that will help you during the journey around the universe, the most important of which are platforms of different functions and sizes, which will make you reconsider how to progress along this. In the same way, you will face challenges that will lead you to feel an extraordinary experience in outer space, and help you on your adventure through the universe. As you progress, you will witness the magnificent art and music that accompanies this experience, equally, you will be able to improve this pleasant trip with all the characters that belong to this universe.
